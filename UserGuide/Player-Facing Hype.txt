README 1 — Player-Facing Hype Version
JavaBarGUI
Run the Night. Own the Chaos.

JavaBarGUI is a deep, systems-driven pub management simulation where every decision ripples through your business.

You’re not just pouring drinks. You’re managing reputation, staff morale, regulatory risk, VIP loyalty, seasonal demand, finances, and chaos — all at once.

Every service is a story.

What You Control

Pricing strategy and supply management

Staff hiring, morale, and progression

Security policies and enforcement

Reputation and public identity

VIP relationships

Trading Standards risk

Inn guest behaviour and hospitality incidents

Financial survival: wages, rent, fines, debt, and profit

What Makes It Different

This is not a clicker. It’s not a shallow idle loop.

JavaBarGUI is built around cause-and-effect systems:

Serve underage customers? Expect regulatory consequences.

Neglect security? Reputation spirals.

Build strong relationships? Regulars return.

Expand the inn? New revenue — and new problems.

Every mechanic connects.

A Living System

Customers aren’t numbers.
Staff aren’t static modifiers.
Your pub develops an identity over time.

Reputation shapes event frequency.
Security affects risk exposure.
Inn performance changes the tone of your nights.
Milestones unlock new possibilities.

You can play safe.
Or you can push the edge.

The Core Experience

Survive the early nights.
Stabilize your finances.
Balance risk and reward.
Scale your operation.
Build a respected venue — or a notorious one.

One bad week won’t end you.
But repeated bad decisions will.

The doors are open.
What kind of landlord are you going to be?
